How to type in other languages in windows 10 (Steps 11)



Steps:


1. Open settings

2. Click on time & languages settings

3. Click on Language

4. Click on add a language

5. Select your language that you want to type in your computer and click next button

6. Click on install button and the language will be installed

7. As you can see that the language is installed

8. Click on ENG button on your right side of taskbar or press Windows+Space

9. Select your language and start typing

10. If you want to type in english then press Windows+Space and select english

11. If you want to delete a language then go to settings and click on the language and select remove button
